Long Sleeve Tech T
Fast-wicking long sleeved tee made with recycled yarns for daily rotation.

Our Long Sleeve Tech T brings you a super lightweight, highly breathable performance with a clean silhouette and clear conscience. We experimented with a featherweight, recycled mesh to create the ideal piece of kit to form the foundation of your training wardrobe. Its four-panel construction using bonded seams ensures tailoring whilst eliminating potential chafing zones. The addition of elastane in the yarn means that your running form won’t be restricted, so you’ll feel as comfortable on the last mile as you did on the first.

The design is rounded off with signature reflective detailing to keep you seen as the light levels drop and an anti-bacterial treatment to control odours.
