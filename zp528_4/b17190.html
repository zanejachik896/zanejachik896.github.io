Run Shorts
Versatile, technical mid-length shorts for every day runs.

The Run Shorts bring high performance to the everyday in a lightweight, breezy package. Cut in a relaxed fit with superior breathability, these mid-thigh shorts are designed to perform across a broad range of distances and intensities.

We allied a featherweight technical fabric with laser-cut ventilation holes at the sides to boost breathability, a bonded hem for even greater comfort, notched hems on the outer leg for freedom of movement, and an ultra-luxe brief that wicks and breathes exactly as it should. What else? There’s also a zipped pocket on the rear to stow on-run carry, like phone and keys.

In combination, it all adds up to one thing: go and enjoy a better Run.

Pair any pair of Run Shorts with any Long Sleeve Tech T to complete a staple spring training system and save 15%
