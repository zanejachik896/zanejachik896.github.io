Crop Race Vest
A lightweight, breathable cropped vest engineered for speed and cut for racing. Made with 100% recycled fabric.

The sister garment to our Race Vest, this cropped version combines speed-session functionality with our signature ultra-light SPACE3D mesh for unmatched breathability and a tailored cut for the lightest and airiest feels when running your fastest.

Cut with our trimmest fit to date, it borrows many of the same design cues as found in our proven Race Vest, the bonded neckline and arm holes boost comfort and eliminate chafing. Around the top of the chest and under the bust line, we’ve gone for more streamlined tailoring, making a tighter fit for a racier feel and closing it off with a bonded bottom hem that feels good on the skin.

At just 20g in a size S not a gram has been wasted in our pursuit to make this a favourite at speed.

Laser-Cut
Minimises the weight of the garment, improves comfort and prevents the fraying of hems.

Ultra Lightweight
Featherweight fabric and minimised construction ensures you hardly notice the garment on the run.

Constructed from fabrics that have been repurposed.
