A blend of 52% Pinot Noir, 27% Chardonnay and 21% Pinot Meunier, with 14% of red wine then added, the Moët & Chandon 2015 Extra Brut Grand Vintage Rosé is showing very well, evoking aromas of red berries, spring flowers, mandarin, cherries and pastry. Enveloping and full-bodied, it’s fruity and fresh, its gastronomic mid-palate segueing into a sappy finish.
(The Wine Advocate, Apr 21, 2023)
